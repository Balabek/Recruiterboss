Job Description

This role offers an exciting opportunity to be part of the Fidelity Market Data History & Analytics Product team, working closely with the entire Market Data team, delivering new and enhanced solutions to the Fidelity Market Data History & Analytics product suite. As a Software Engineer, you will be a technical contributor and part of a team responsible for developing and maintaining our History & Analytics System using a wide range of technologies, predominately JAVA based. The Fidelity Market Data group acquires and delivers Financial Market Data to Fidelity Business Units and Customer via different channels. The system which delivers the data is primarily a REST web service, returning XML/JSON to downstream clients.

Primary Responsibilities

  • Software engineer able to develop/code technical solutions using a variety of technologies particularly JAVA
  • Designing and developing enterprise scale Market Data solutions providing data to enterprise applications
  • Work as a member of a small team on the development, unit testing and integration testing of all changes to the Fidelity Market Data History & Analytics product suite
  • Recommending system solutions by comparing advantages of current development tools/methods vs. alternate solutions/technologies/methodology and working independently and/or with technical partners to design and document the technical specifications
  • Translate business requirements into implementation process by considering all alternatives and implementing the best strategy to ensure design meets performance, usability, reliability, and scalability requirements
